About

Welcome to Marie Dishes, where our passion for authentic Kenyan cuisine meets a commitment to community and convenience. Founded with a love for sharing the flavors of home, we're delighted to bring a taste of Kenya's culinary heritage to your doorstep.
At Marie Dishes, we believe in making delicious food accessible to all. That's why we're thrilled to offer free delivery for our esteemed MNUC comrades.
From the bustling streets of Mutomo to of Bold-Shepherd, we're dedicated to ensuring that no one misses out on the joy of our delectable dishes. Our commitment to quality extends beyond the kitchen, the services are woven into every aspect of our service.
Whether you're craving the comforting embrace of ugali or the savory delight of omena, rest assured that your meal will be prepared with the utmost care and attention to detail.
Join us on a journey of culinary exploration and community connection. Whether you're dining in or enjoying the convenience of our free delivery service, we're here to make every experience unforgettable. Thank you for choosing Marie Dishes – where good food and great company come together in perfect harmony.
